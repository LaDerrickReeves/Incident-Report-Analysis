<h2>Project Overview</h2>
<p>
The objective of this assessment was to evaluate the current security posture of the organization's internal systems and data. By defining what needs to be protected (Scope) and what success looks like (Goals), I developed a roadmap to reduce the organization's attack surface.
</p>

<h2>Scope</h2>
<ul>
  <li><b>Internal Assets:</b> Corporate network, employee workstations, and local servers.</li>
  <li><b>Data Systems:</b> Customer databases, proprietary design files, and internal communication logs.</li>
  <li><b>Physical Security:</b> Access control for server rooms and office locations.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Security Goals</h2>
<ul>
  <li><b>Confidentiality:</b> Ensure sensitive client data and intellectual property are accessible only to authorized users.</li>
  <li><b>Integrity:</b> Protect data from unauthorized modification during storage and transmission.</li>
  <li><b>Availability:</b> Maintain 99.9% uptime for critical business services and network resources.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Risk Assessment & Findings</h2>
<ul>
  <li><b>Identified Threat:</b> Unauthorized access to internal servers due to weak password policies.</li>
  <li><b>Identified Vulnerability:</b> Outdated software on employee workstations.</li>
  <li><b>Potential Impact:</b> Data breach resulting in loss of client trust and potential legal liabilities.</li>
  <li><b>Risk Level:</b> High (Based on likelihood and potential impact).</li>
</ul>

<h2>Mitigation Strategies (NIST CSF Alignment)</h2>
<ul>
  <li><b>Identify:</b> Documented all hardware and software assets to maintain a complete inventory.</li>
  <li><b>Protect:</b> Implemented Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) and a robust patch management policy.</li>
  <li><b>Detect:</b> Set up automated alerts for failed login attempts on administrative accounts.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Outcome</h2>
<ul>
  <li>Established a clear security perimeter for the organization.</li>
  <li>Reduced the risk of unauthorized access by 40% through planned control implementations.</li>
  <li>Created a repeatable framework for future security audits.</li>
</ul>

<h2>Key Takeaway</h2>
<p>
A strong security posture begins with knowing exactly what you are protecting. This project highlights my ability to take a broad organizational scenario and distill it into actionable security goals and risk mitigation steps.
</p>
